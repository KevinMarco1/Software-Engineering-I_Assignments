Name: book seat. 
Actor: web customer 
Description: helps the customer to book the performance he/she want. 
precondition: get into web page 
postcondition:  customer booked some performance.

Flow of events: 
1.    Customers open the web page
2.    Customer will choose the icon of book seat and get into the view performances
3.    The customer will have some options and will choose to explore 
4.    The customer will choose the type of performance he prefers
5.    Customer will choose the performance he likes
6.    Customer will be login in and pay for performance by credit card or loyalty points 
7.    Customer will check out




Special requirements: when the customer click at book seat icon there is a screen will be
			appeared dividing the genre of the performances he prefers.
